Issue 4, Sep 2006

In a word, Fashion.

Ever since Socrates and his Method, it’s been fashionable to ask questions. In this issue we’re asking questions about Fashion itself. What is it? Why did we start worrying about it? How do you become a fashion designer?

You can download the entire issue as a PDF (921KB), or browse through the articles below. To view or print the PDF, you’ll need Adobe Reader.

If you’re keen, have a listen to the audio recording of the full interview (MP3, 6.7MB) with fashion designer Francis Hooper from WORLD. (Please note: this is unedited, and some of the language is not for the faint of heart.)
